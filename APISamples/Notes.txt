You can see this hosted at:
http://jennmlin.com/funnel.html?company=getsatisfaction

The only parameter to pass at the command line is the community name. This
allows for looking at data across any public community.

The topics are from the last week and sorted by status.

The page uses HTML5 to render the visual elements, as it makes the little
widget a bit more fun. However, nothing in the canvas rendered section of 
the widget is truly critical functionally, so feel free to take the 
inspiration and apply it to non-HTML5 environments.
